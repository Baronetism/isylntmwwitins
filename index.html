Made by one fan, twenty years deep. I was ten when The Black Parade taught me that grief could sound like a brass band and a battle cry at the same time — it has been my favorite record ever since, and I don’t expect that to change. This domain was just sitting there, free. It felt like the loudest way to say the quietest thing. Every ounce of credit belongs to My Chemical Romance — the band, the album, the song, and Gerard Way, who gave so many of us somewhere to put it all.
