the story

When I was little, I used to put my headphones on and listen to Dead! when my parents were fighting — because Gerard could scream louder than they could, and somehow his screaming never scared me.

Ever since I was ten years old, The Black Parade has been my favorite album. And here I am, twenty years later, watching the band play it live — on my honeymoon.

I am so grateful for everything this band has done for me. They have been there in my toughest moments, and they gave me so much hope and so much happiness.

“ISYLNTMWWITINS” is the first line of Famous Last Words — I see you lying next to me, with words I thought I’d never speak. Every ounce of credit belongs to My Chemical Romance, and to Gerard Way. not affiliated · just grateful · forever
